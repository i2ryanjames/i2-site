What is WISE Global?

A ministry management system for the global church

The WISE Global is a ministry management system focused on reaching the largest unreached peoples with the Gospel. It helps denominations, churches, and missions organizations launch and track the progress of prayer, training, and evangelism initiatives.

WISE has a simple, user-friendly interface available in multiple languages, allowing users to make commitments in prayer, training, and evangelism, form groups, access video training, and pin the location of their ministry activity. These features integrate with a progress dashboard tracking personal, small group, church, denominational, regional, and national ministry progress.

As a free resource for the global church, WISE contains over 250 hours of video training from 20+ courses in 15 languages by globally recognized professors on Christian Missions to Muslims and Islamic Studies.

App Features

Everything you need to coordinate outreach

Join the initiative by making a personal commitment across three pillars: prayer, training other believers, and sharing the gospel. Report your progress in each area and track it through an intuitive dashboard showing personal, small group, church, denominational, regional, and national ministry progress — all in real time.

Create and join secure groups within your church, denomination, or ministry network. WISE includes group and direct messaging with encrypted communication so teams can coordinate safely — even in sensitive regions. View member commitment progress and follow up with group members directly inside the app.

Access over 250 hours of professionally produced video training from world-renowned professors. The easy-to-navigate training environment tracks session progress and course completion. Professionally dubbed in 15 languages including English, Arabic, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Twi (Ghanaian), Korean, Mandarin, Telugu, Bahasa Indonesian, Urdu, Swahili, and Amharic. Pick up where you left off and stream even in low-bandwidth areas.

Download video sessions and entire courses for training in areas with little or no internet connectivity. The WISE ministry management system is fully functional even without a network connection — report commitments, track discipleship, and manage groups offline. All changes sync automatically when you're back online. This feature was specifically built to serve partners like the Church of Pentecost in Ghana who operate in remote villages and unreached areas.

Track those who come to Christ by creating individual profiles for new disciples. Record their religious background, monitor their growth journey, and disciple converts to maturity. Keep a comprehensive record of all your disciples in one place.

Collaborate to reach every household in your area. Pin the locations of outreach activities on an interactive map, view outreach details, identify and map strategic places, and add notes about strategic locations for your team. See where outreaches are happening in real time.

WISE creates a personalized dashboard for each denomination to measure and track the progress of their initiative with intuitive graphs and statistics. Leaders can view where outreaches occurred in real-time, monitor outreach statistics, track how many came to Christ, track discipleship progress, view strategic places, and access easy-to-read graphs and reports.

The full user interface is available in 7 languages: English, Español, Français, Português, አማርኛ (Amharic), Swahili, and Arabic (عربي). This ensures that leaders and church members around the world can navigate the entire app in their heart language.

In the Field

WISE in action around the world

Ghana: Church of Pentecost

After presenting the WISE Global app to 5,000 pastors (with 40,000 more watching online), the Church of Pentecost officially adopted WISE as part of their 5-year agenda. They appointed 77 area coordinators, 3,000 district coordinators, and 28,000 local coordinators — all using WISE. In just over a year and a half, more than 10,500 Muslims came to Christ.

Tanzania: Assemblies of God

All 71 bishops of the Tanzania Assemblies of God agreed to run "Every Muslim for Christ" conferences in their regions using the WISE Global app, translated into Swahili. Their goal: reach all 24 million Muslims in their country.

Latin America: 6,851 Churches Mobilizing

An immigrant bricklayer in Florida funded two courses overdubbed into Spanish. A decade later, 6,851 churches across the Latin world are now using those courses through WISE to train laborers to reach the Muslim world.
