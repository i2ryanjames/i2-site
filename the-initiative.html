The Urgency

The largest unreached people on the planet

Muslims represent the largest unreached people group on the planet. Over 38,000 die every day without the gospel. Islam has grown from 230 million in 1908 to nearly 2 billion today — and is projected to reach 2.8 billion by 2050, nearing parity with Christianity.

86% of Muslims have never personally known a Christian. Only 1 missionary serves every 450,000 Muslims. 97% of all missionaries work among peoples who already have gospel access. The church is skipping the evangelization of Muslims.

The greatest problem in the world is not radical Islam — it is nominal Christianity. The answer to global Islam is a discipled and trained Church that loves Jesus!
— Joshua Lingel, President of i2 Ministries
The Strategic Plan

How we reach every Muslim for Christ

The EMFCI is a three-pronged mega strategy that partners with the largest denominations in each country to launch national prayer, training, evangelism, and discipleship initiatives among Muslims.

Together with denominational leaders, we launch a national prayer, training, evangelism, and discipleship initiative among Muslims. i2 partners with senior denominational leadership — not just individual churches — to ensure the vision cascades through thousands of congregations. Examples include the Church of Pentecost in Ghana (27,000+ churches across 170 countries), the Tanzania Assemblies of God (16,000 churches), and IDAMCE across 7 Central American nations (6,851 churches).

We help create a plan to train every church by setting up national, regional, and local church launch conferences. Each conference equips 1,000+ pastors and leaders to mobilize their congregations to reach every Muslim in their region. These are not one-time events — they are designed as "launch conferences" that show leaders how to multiply the training long after the i2 team is gone.

We offer 30 video courses equipping churches to reach Muslims for Jesus Christ, available in English, Arabic, Urdu, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Telugu, Indonesian, Swahili, Amharic, Twi, Korean, and Mandarin. We never introduce training into a country that the national churches cannot replicate and scale into all their churches.

We provide up to 1,000 scholarships per country into Mission Muslim World University, which offers the most comprehensive curriculum globally in Christian Missions to Muslims and Islamic Studies — with a pathway to a fully accredited MA degree. Hundreds of pastors and leaders from 40+ countries have received full scholarships and today lead 'Every Muslim for Christ' initiatives in their nations.

The WISE Global phone app is a powerful integrative ministry management system that allows denominations, churches, and missions organizations to launch and track the progress of prayer, training, evangelism, and discipleship initiatives. Every Christian in every church can join the initiative and track their individual and group progress. Leaders get personalized dashboards with real-time statistics.

We set up a customized logistical support system, website dashboards for each denomination to track missions progress from the field, and provide ongoing coaching to overcome obstacles. Upon our recommendation, a National Coordinator is appointed within each denomination to drive the initiative across all regions.

Impact Stories

Where the Initiative is already working

Ghana: 30,000+ Muslims Won for Christ

The Church of Pentecost officially adopted the EMFCI as part of their 5-year agenda. They appointed 77 area coordinators, 3,000 district coordinators, and 28,000 local coordinators. In just over 18 months, more than 10,500 Muslims came to Christ — and over 30,000 since 2017.

Tanzania: 16,000 Churches Mobilized

Archbishop Barnabas Mtokambali brought his executive committee of 400 top leaders — 71 bishops and all assistants — for an EMFCI launch conference. All 71 bishops agreed to run "Every Muslim for Christ Conferences" in their regions across the entire denomination.

7 Nations, 6,851 Churches Training

IDAMCE — the Church of God denomination spanning Belize, Guatemala, El Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and Panama — enrolled all area superintendents, bishops, and key ministry directors into MMWU training. Nearly 10,000 ministers now have access to tools they've never had before.
